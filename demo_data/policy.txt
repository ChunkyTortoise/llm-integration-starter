CloudSync Pro Company Policies

RETURN AND REFUND POLICY
- All subscription plans include a 14-day free trial with full feature access.
- Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time; service continues until the end of the billing period.
- Annual subscriptions are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
- After 30 days, annual subscription refunds are prorated based on remaining months.
- Enterprise custom contracts follow terms specified in the signed agreement.

PRIVACY POLICY
- We collect only the data necessary to provide our synchronization services.
- Customer data in transit is never stored on our servers beyond the sync operation.
- Account information (email, company name, billing) is stored securely with AES-256 encryption.
- We never sell or share customer data with third parties for marketing purposes.
- Customers can request full data export or deletion at any time via support@cloudsync.io.
- We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and SOC 2 Type II requirements.

ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
- CloudSync Pro must not be used to synchronize illegal or prohibited content.
- Customers must not attempt to bypass rate limits or abuse API endpoints.
- Automated scripts must include proper error handling and respect rate limit headers.
- Sharing API credentials or access tokens with unauthorized parties is prohibited.
- Violation of these policies may result in account suspension or termination.

SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
- CloudSync Pro guarantees 99.9% uptime for Professional and Enterprise plans.
- Starter plans target 99.5% uptime but are not covered by SLA credits.
- Scheduled maintenance windows are announced 72 hours in advance.
- In the event of SLA breach, affected customers receive service credits.
- Credit amounts: <99.9% = 10% credit, <99.5% = 25% credit, <99.0% = 50% credit.

DATA RETENTION
- Sync job execution logs are retained for 90 days on all plans.
- Enterprise customers can configure custom retention periods up to 7 years.
- Deleted sync jobs and their history are permanently removed after 30 days.
- Account data is retained for 60 days after account closure for recovery purposes.
